Dr. Linda Young, DNP

My care philosophy has been developed and refined over my years of practice. I believe caring for patients is a privilege, not a right given to me because of my degrees and education. I believe that everyone belongs to someone and I take the time to care for my patients as I would someone that belongs to me. I take the time to listen to my patients so I can understand and recognize their health needs and address the issues that they may be struggling with. I believe in building a long-term patient-provider relationship based on trust, skill, and timely advice.
